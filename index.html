My Professional Approach to Mental Health

I believe in a holistic approach to psychological counseling that mixes proven therapeutic methods with kind, personalized care. My experiences studying and working in the Middle East and North America give me a global view in my practice.


With a diverse educational background in psychology, NLP, and psychotherapy, I combine professional psychological techniques with a spiritual perspective that respects the whole person. This mix creates a safe, non-judgmental space where clients can explore their thoughts, feelings, and actions.


Whether you're facing anxiety, depression, relationship problems, or personal growth goals, my international and spiritual approach equips you with the tools to handle life's challenges with confidence and resilience. This helps you clear your mind and find clarity on your path forward.
